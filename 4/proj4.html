Project 4: Image Mosaicing

Part 1: Shooting and Digitizing Pictures

In this section, I captured and digitized photographs with overlapping fields of view to ensure effective image mosaicing. The goal was to create a sequence of images with projective transformations by rotating the camera and fixing the center of projection.

Photograph 2

Part 2: Recovering Homographies

I computed homographies between image pairs using manually defined point correspondences and solved the linear system using least-squares to obtain the transformation matrix. The homographies allow precise warping between images.

Homography Recovery

Part 3: Warping the Images

Using the recovered homographies, I warped images to align with the reference image. I implemented forward and inverse warping techniques, ensuring proper resampling and handling of aliasing.
